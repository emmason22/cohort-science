About David

David Rheams, PhD

Associate

A practitioner at the intersection of academic research, corporate strategy, and product innovation. Holds a PhD in Cultural Studies from George Mason University, with a focus on the intersection of science, software, and communication. Over 15 years of startup leadership experience at companies including LTK and ReachLocal, with a consistent record of scaling operations while maintaining a people-first approach.
