The Shift

Higher education is undergoing a fundamental structural transformation.

Demographic shifts, credential disruption, workforce realignment, and evolving capital flows are redefining how institutions grow, how investors evaluate the sector, and how consulting firms serve their clients.

Cohort Science™ is purpose-built for this purpose, providing the analytical depth and speed of delivery that consequential decisions demand.

The Idea

A new foundation for education intelligence.

Cohort Science™ maintains a continuously updated intelligence base spanning labor markets, student demand, program economics, graduate outcomes, and geographic market dynamics.

Our proprietary econometric framework evaluates 40 to 60 variables across labor market conditions, student demand signals, career value trajectories, and competitive positioning, producing market intelligence that is rigorous, reproducible, and ready when decisions need to be made.

How Our Products Help Clients

Four products. One platform. Built for the decisions that matter.

Market Signal

Predict market demand for programs.

  • Forecast enrollment and revenue for new and existing degree programs
  • Identify high-yield student segments by demographic, geography, and program type
  • Model the impact of aid policy, tuition changes, and marketing investment on yield
  • Analyze competitive positioning across institutional and geographic markets
  • Rapid market sizing for consulting engagements and investment due diligence

Dean's Workbench

Simulate and predict trends among student classes and clusters.

  • Analyze behavior trends of student clusters across enrollment, retention, and progression
  • Model downstream effects of scheduling, advising capacity, and support resource allocation
  • Simulate interventions and forecast impact prior to deployment
  • Ground institutional decisions in evidence and reproducible analysis

Economic Mobility and Pathways

Connect programs to real-world outcomes.

  • Track graduate earnings and employment at institution, degree, and class level
  • Identify which programs drive long-term economic mobility and which warrant review
  • Map employer relationships and alumni networks to strengthen institutional partnerships
  • Align program design with evolving labor market demand

Impact Audit

Independent, investor-grade evaluation.

  • Assess grant effectiveness, program ROI, and vendor performance objectively
  • Evaluate outcomes-based accreditation evidence with analytical rigor
  • Identify high-performing areas and opportunities for strategic reallocation
  • Purpose-built for consulting firms, foundations, and investors needing fast-turnaround intelligence briefs

Clients We Serve

Sector intelligence tailored to each decision-maker.

Universities and Colleges

Private and public colleges, faith-based institutions, and community colleges rely on Cohort Science™ for a clear, continuous picture of student movement from recruitment through graduation. Leaders use our platform to grow enrollment, close equity gaps, and demonstrate outcomes to boards and accreditors with confidence.

Venture Capital and Private Equity Firms

Investment decisions in higher education require market intelligence that matches the pace of capital deployment. Cohort Science™ provides program-level demand analysis, institutional health assessments, and competitive market positioning for investment committees and due diligence processes.

Foundations and Philanthropists

Effective philanthropy requires rigorous measurement. Cohort Science™ provides independent, outcomes-based evaluation of program performance, tracking earnings, mobility, and institutional impact to inform allocation decisions with evidence rather than self-reported data.

Management Consulting Firms

Education practices at strategy and investment consulting firms rely on timely, rigorous sector intelligence to serve clients effectively. Cohort Science™ functions as a dedicated intelligence partner, compressing research timelines and strengthening analytical depth.

Non-Profit and Mission-Driven Organizations

Organizations expanding access and improving outcomes for underserved student populations require data rigorous enough for funders and boards. Cohort Science™ provides the analytical foundation to demonstrate social return on investment with credibility.

Government and Policy-makers

Education policy-makers at federal, state, and local levels use Cohort Science™ for segment-level analysis of socioeconomic trends, connecting education investments to workforce outcomes and informing funding priorities with evidence-based insight.
