About Lauren

Lauren Jensen

Partner

Lauren Jensen is an executive leader in higher education with proven success in enrollment, financial aid, planning and analytics. As Founding Principal of ARC Insights, LLC, she advises executives on strategy and measurable results. Her work in enrollment and analytics as VP for Enrollment Strategy at Calvin University (MI) and Thompson Rivers University (BC), led to repeated enrollment growth and institutional revenue gains.

Lauren joined Cohort Science in 2026 and brings over twenty years of higher education leadership experience in public and private universities. Lauren is passionate about helping education organizations and senior leaders develop effective strategies to navigate the landscape of higher education and enrollment.
