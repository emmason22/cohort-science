About Pavan

Pavan Kumar

Advisor

Dr. Pavan is a researcher, entrepreneur, and business-builder. He recently completed his doctorate at the Erik Jonsson School of Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas. Dr. Pavan has been a Graduate Fellow in the prestigious Archer Fellowship Program, which develops the next generation of leaders and problem-solvers for local, state, federal, and international service from The University of Texas System.

Dr. Pavan's doctoral research was aimed at the investigation, alignment, measurement, and prediction of Engineering Identity of engineering undergraduates. In this work, Dr. Pavan studied how Engineering Identity evolves in students, and the various influences in the university shaping it.

Dr. Pavan is a fervent advocate of reforms in higher education in STEM disciplines in the US. He has worked with Governments and Industry at various levels to implement systemic changes to better support STEM education and the workforce. During his doctoral studies, Dr. Pavan served as a Research Assistant in programs focused on AI and automation in manufacturing.

Earlier, Dr. Pavan worked in technical and managerial roles, and founded and directed several high-impact organizations in India. Dr. Pavan has been a champion in the Maker Movement in India, which paved the way for many successful startups. In his last venture in India, Dr. Pavan led a billionaire philanthropist's nonprofit organization dedicated to innovations and impact in the healthcare sector.
