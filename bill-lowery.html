About Bill

Bill Lowery

Managing Partner

Joining Cohort Science in 2024, Bill brings a wealth of experience and a proven track record in helping colleges and universities grow their enrollments. With a deep passion for education, he excels in identifying and overcoming barriers to the expansion of affordable, high-quality, and workforce-relevant education.

Bill has held senior leadership positions at some of the world’s leading higher education companies. His strategic use of data-driven insights and innovative solutions enhances enrollment growth, student experiences, and educational outcomes.
