About Garima

Garima Singh

Associate

Garima brings over five years of investment banking experience to her work at Cohort Science, where she serves as Associate of Strategy and Analysis. Earlier in her career, she worked in managerial roles and collaborated with successful startups, building a sharp instinct for both structure and agility. Her background spans global equity markets, credit ratings, and quantitative research, work that demanded both deep financial rigor and the ability to deliver insights under pressure.

At Cohort Science, Garima has built and owns the company's economic mobility analysis product, designing the models, dashboards, and workflows that help clients understand where they stand and chart a smarter path forward. She partners closely with clients to untangle complex datasets, surface growth opportunities, and translate analytical findings into strategies that drive measurable impact.

Garima holds an MBA in Financial Market Practices and recently completed her Master of Science in Business Analytics and AI from the University of Texas at Dallas, a combination that reflects her dual command of financial markets and modern data science.
